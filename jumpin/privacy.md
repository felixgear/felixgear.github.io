# Privacy Policy - JumpIn

**Last updated:** 2026-02-05

JumpIn is an offline Hungarian word game. The app works without user accounts and without any backend service.

## Data we collect
JumpIn does **not collect** personal data or information that identifies you.

- No account registration
- No name, email, phone number collection
- No location data
- No device identifiers collected
- No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs used (as of this version)

## Data stored on your device
JumpIn may store a small amount of data **locally on your device** to provide gameplay features, such as:
- selected difficulty setting
- daily limits state (e.g., remaining hint/skip tokens)
- local game progress and scores (e.g., best score)

This data stays on your device and is not shared with the developer or third parties.

## Network access
JumpIn does not require network access to function and does not send gameplay or personal data to any server.
